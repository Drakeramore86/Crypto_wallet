Crypto Card FAQs

A crypto card allows you to spend cryptocurrencies like you would with a traditional debit or credit card. It converts your digital assets into the local currency at the time of the transaction.

The card is linked to your VirtualCoin Wallet Card.com account. When you make a purchase, funds are automatically converted from cryptocurrency to fiat currency to complete the transaction.

You can order your card through the VirtualCoin Wallet Card.com app. Just verify your identity, provide a shipping address, and your card will be on its way.
